The History of Red Vox

Red Vox is a band from Staten Island, New York City. This page will provide info on their history.
The page on music will provide deeper dives into their music

Before Red Vox

Before Red Vox was created, Vinny was in a different band called Davy's Grey with Mike and another guy named Phil.
That band broke up in the early 2010s, and the members did other band stuff for a while. Sometime in 2014-2015, Vinny and Mike decided to get back together to form a band. The band couldnt figure out a name for a while, until Vinny saw the words "Red Vox" on a poster in a dream.

2015-2016

The first released Red Vox song, There She Goes, was released on May 19, 2015. It was the first song from the album What Could Go Wrong. During 2015 and early 2016, several more singles from the album were released. On October 22nd, 2015 the single Trolls and Goblins was released. It was a charity incentive from the Vinesauce Is Hope charity streams. The album was then released on March 29, 2016. On April 10th, they did a live show at John and Peter's. They also did one at TooManyGames 2016. On September 14th, 2016, the comedy EP Blood Bagel was released. It is a departure from their normal music. It was not the next real album however. Behind the Bagel, a behind the scenes video, was released on September 23rd, 2016.

2017-2018

On March 16th 2017, the single In The Garden was released. It became their most popular song on streaming services. The next single, Reno, was released on July 2nd, 2017. Vinny got drunk before singing it allegedly. The album had also been delayed to record another song. Another Light finally released on December 14th, 2017. For the vinyl release of Another Light, a new song called Stranded was added. It is their longest song at 9 minutes. Another Light demos were also released on Bandcamp on October 5th, 2018.

2019-2020

On January 8th, the album Kerosene was released It is a departure from the typical sound, as it is acoustic. On October 31st 2019, the first single from the album Realign was released. Throughout 2020, various singles for Realign were released. Ozymandias was released on Feburary 20th, 2020. The title track Realign was released on June 25th, 2020. The album was released on July 9th, 2020. A behind the scenes video called The Realign Sessions was released on July 15th, 2020. An ep called Lost for A While was released on October 29th, 2020.

2021-2022

There wasnt much music released during 2021. On October 28th, 2021, two new tracks from the new album Visions were released. They were There's a Place and Ellesar. On January 6th, 2022 Choking on the Spite released. 7 days later, Visions came out. On May 6th, 2022 Jumped the Gun from Afterthoughts was released. Various more singles were released throughout 2022 and 2023.

2023-2024

The album Afterthoughts was released on March 2nd, 2023. They secretly did a show on September 30th, 2023. This was revealed later. On April 1st, 2024, Garbage Land was released. 6 days later, Clone Me released. This was the Garbage Land EP. The new single for the next album, Remember was released on June 26th, 2024. They did a TooManyGames show on the 29th. They are doing another show on the 30th of November

2025

They were announced to be doing a show at Magfest on an unknown date in January. A new album may be released this year.
