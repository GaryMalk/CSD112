Live Shows

This page will contain info on all the live shows that the band has done, including future shows.

John & Peter's

The first live show the band did was at John & Peter's in New Hope, Pennsylvania.
The tracklist is unknown, but Atom Bomb was played there. There isn't much known about this one.

TooManyGames 2016

The second live show was at TooManyGames 2016.
The tracklist is
There She Goes
Back To School
Job In The City
Hazy
We Had A Little Talk
In A Dream
Outtasite (Outta Mind) by Wilco (cover)
Fuck
Scoot The Burbs
Trolls and Goblins
Ghost Page
Atom Bomb
Fun Fact: Mike lost one of his drumsticks during Fuck.

The Hoppe Shoppe

The third live show was at the Hoppe Shoppe on Staten Island in 2023. This was a test to see if they could do live shows. Only the song Reno is known to have been played there. Not many videos exist from this show.

Upcoming live shows
