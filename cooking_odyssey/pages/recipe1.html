Cosmic Veggie Stir-Fry

A quick and colorful veggie stir-fry with a tangy soy-ginger sauce that's perfect for weeknights.

Ingredients

							1 tbsp olive oil
							1 bell pepper, sliced
							1 carrot, julienned
							1 zucchini, sliced
							1 cup broccoli florets
							1/4 cup soy sauce
							1 tbsp ginger, minced
							1 tbsp garlic, minced
							1 tsp sesame seeds

Directions

							1.	Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
							2.	Add minced garlic and ginger, sautéing for about 1 minute until fragrant.
							3.	Toss in the bell pepper, carrot, zucchini, and broccoli. Stir-fry for 5-7 minutes or until vegetables are tender-crisp.
							4.	Pour in the soy sauce, stirring everything to coat evenly.
							5.	Sprinkle sesame seeds on top, give it one last stir, and serve warm. Enjoy your veggie adventure!

							Nutrition Facts (per serving):
							•	Protein: 6g
							•	Fat: 5g
							•	Carbohydrates: 15g
							•	Calories: 120
