Interstellar Salmon Bowl

A hearty bowl of salmon, rice, and veggies, drizzled with a lemon-herb sauce.

Ingredients

							•	1 salmon fillet (4 oz)
							•	1/2 cup cooked brown rice
							•	1/4 cup broccoli, steamed
							•	1/4 cup carrots, steamed
							•	1 tbsp lemon juice
							•	1 tsp dill

Directions

						1.	Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
						2.	Place the salmon fillet on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
						3.	Drizzle lemon juice over the salmon and sprinkle with dill.
						4.	Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the salmon flakes easily with a fork.
						5.	Meanwhile, prepare your bowl by adding cooked brown rice, steamed broccoli, and steamed carrots.
						6.	Place the baked salmon on top, and your interstellar fuel is ready to go!

						Nutrition Facts (per bowl):
						•	Protein: 25g
						•	Fat: 12g
						•	Carbohydrates: 30g
						•	Calories: 350
