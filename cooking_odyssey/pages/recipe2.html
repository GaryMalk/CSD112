Space Traveler's Chicken Wrap

A protein-packed chicken wrap with creamy avocado and crunchy veggies, ideal for a meal on the go.

Ingredients

							•	1 whole-wheat tortilla
							•	1/2 cup cooked chicken breast, shredded
							•	1/4 avocado, sliced
							•	1/4 cup lettuce, chopped
							•	1/4 cup cucumber, sliced
							•	1 tbsp Greek yogurt
							•	Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

							1.	Lay the whole-wheat tortilla flat on a clean surface.
							2.	Spread Greek yogurt across the center.
							3.	Layer shredded chicken, avocado, lettuce, and cucumber evenly on the tortilla.
							4.	Season with salt and pepper to taste.
							5.	Roll up the tortilla, tucking in the edges as you go, and wrap it tightly.
							6.	Slice in half if desired, and take it with you on your next interstellar journey!
	
							Nutrition Facts (per wrap):
							•	Protein: 22g
							•	Fat: 8g
							•	Carbohydrates: 20g
							•	Calories: 240
