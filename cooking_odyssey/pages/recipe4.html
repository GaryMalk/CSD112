Galactic Greek Salad

A fresh, vibrant salad with a zesty vinaigrette, featuring classic Mediterranean ingredients.

Ingredients

							•	1 cup cucumber, chopped
							•	1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
							•	1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
							•	1/4 cup Kalamata olives, pitted
							•	1 tbsp olive oil
							•	1 tbsp lemon juice
							•	Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

						1.	In a large bowl, combine chopped cucumber, cherry tomatoes, feta cheese, and Kalamata olives.
						2.	Drizzle olive oil and lemon juice over the salad.
						3.	Season with salt and pepper to taste.
						4.	Gently toss everything together until evenly coated.
						5.	Serve immediately and savor the fresh Mediterranean vibes!

						Nutrition Facts (per serving):
						•	Protein: 5g
						•	Fat: 10g
						•	Carbohydrates: 10g
						•	Calories: 120
