Martian Meatballs

Juicy and flavorful meatballs with a hint of garlic and herbs, baked to perfection.

Ingredients

							•	1 lb ground beef or turkey
							•	1/4 cup breadcrumbs
							•	1 egg
							•	1 tsp garlic powder
							•	1 tsp Italian seasoning
							•	Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

						1.	Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
						2.	In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground beef or turkey, breadcrumbs, egg, garlic powder, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.
						3.	Shape the mixture into small meatballs, about 1 inch in diameter.
						4.	Place the meatballs on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
						5.	Bake for 15-20 minutes or until browned and cooked through.
						6.	Serve hot and enjoy your Martian morsels!

						Nutrition Facts (per 4 meatballs):
						•	Protein: 18g
						•	Fat: 12g
						•	Carbohydrates: 10g
						•	Calories: 200
