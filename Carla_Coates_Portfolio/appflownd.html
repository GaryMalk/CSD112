Interactive artistic Music platform
A music streaming platform that acts like a social network. Musicians upload their music and fans can express what they feel through their own artworks and photography. “Music is a universal language.”

General target Audience Age Range: 18-35 years Interests: Visual arts, video creation, photography, motion graphics, and multimedia projects.

Ideation and Sketching
Rounded, sans-serif typeface is friendly and approachable. It says the appis user-friendly and modern. The logo gives off a fun and lively vibe, as an energetic and dynamic experience. Playful Scalable IconHigh Contrast

Campaign: Pop up store and merchandise
At the pop-up store users can buy their magazine and merchandise for flownd. Users can search for a playlist on the website and enter a code from their event ticket to specify their order goes to the venue. Their design gets put on the LP and gets signed by an artist(s) that are at the concert or music festival. At the event, when the ticket gets scanned, they will receive a ticket for the pop-up store so they can verify they have an order to pick up. Consumers can pick up their orders. The pop-up store will be at music festivals/events. The artist can pick a favorite design and the consumer can win a prize. An example could be a meet and greet or a special item. The artist receives a portion of revenue from flownd. The pop-up store can make its revenue at the event by selling its own merchandise so users can spread the word and support the cause.
