Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 1, 2025

At The Forged Collection, your privacy matters. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you interact with us—such as taking a quiz, signing up for emails, contacting us, or purchasing products. We never sell your data.

Information We Collect

  • Contact info: name and email when you sign up, contact us, or complete a quiz.
  • Quiz responses & preferences: used to personalize results and content.
  • Order details: if you make a purchase (e.g., products, amounts, basic transaction info).
  • Device & usage data: pages visited, browser type, and similar analytics via cookies or analytics tools.

How We Use Your Information

  • Send quiz results, updates, and exclusive offers.
  • Personalize content and improve our products and site.
  • Respond to questions or support requests.
  • Maintain security and reliable operation of the website.
  • Send occasional marketing emails (you can opt out anytime).

Sharing Your Information

We only share your information in limited situations:

  • With trusted service providers who help us operate our website, quizzes, payments, or email (e.g., quiz tools, email platforms, payment processors).
  • If required by law, to respond to legal requests, or to protect our rights.

Cookies & Analytics

We may use cookies and similar technologies to remember preferences and understand how visitors use the site. You can control cookies through your browser settings; disabling some cookies may affect site functionality.

Data Retention

We keep personal information only as long as needed for the purposes described in this policy and to comply with legal obligations.

Your Choices

  • Email opt-out: use the unsubscribe link in our emails or contact us to be removed.
  • Update or delete: email us to request updates or deletion of your information where applicable.
  • Do Not Track: we currently do not respond to DNT signals.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Effective date” above reflects the latest revision. Material changes will be highlighted on this page.
