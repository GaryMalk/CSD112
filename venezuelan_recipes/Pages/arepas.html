Simple Arepa Recipe

Arepas are a Venezuelan classic—warm, golden cornmeal patties with a crispy exterior and soft, fluffy inside. They’re endlessly versatile, perfect for filling with cheese, meats, or vegetables, and enjoyed at any time of day.

Preparation Time

  • Total: 20 minutes
  • Preparation: 10 minutes
  • Cooking: 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 cups pre-cooked cornmeal (harina PAN or similar)
  • 2 ½ cups warm water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon butter (plus more for cooking)
  • Cheese, meats, or fillings of choice

Instructions

  1. Mix: In a bowl, combine cornmeal, salt, and warm water. Stir until smooth, then let rest 5 minutes.
  2. Shape: Form small balls and flatten into discs about 1 cm thick.
  3. Cook: Heat a skillet over medium heat with a little oil or butter. Cook arepas 4–5 minutes per side until golden and crispy.
  4. Serve: Slice open and fill with cheese, meats, or desired fillings.
