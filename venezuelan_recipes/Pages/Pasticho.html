Simple Pasticho Recipe

A comforting Venezuelan classic with layers of pasta, rich Bolognese, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese—perfect for sharing with family.

Preparation Time

  • Total: 1 hour 20 minutes
  • Preparation: 30 minutes
  • Cooking: 50 minutes

Ingredients

  • 500 g (1 lb) ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 250 g (9 oz) lasagna noodles (pre-cooked or oven-ready)
  • 500 g (1 lb) mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 200 g (7 oz) Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Ham

Instructions

  1. Cook the beef: In a large pan over medium heat, cook ground beef and onion until browned. Drain excess fat.
  2. Add sauce: Stir in tomato sauce, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  3. Assemble: In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce, then add noodles, more sauce, and mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers, finishing with sauce and both cheeses on top.
  4. Bake: Cover with foil and bake at 180°C (350°F) for 30 minutes. Remove foil and bake 10–15 minutes more until cheese is golden and bubbly.
  5. Serve: Let rest for 10 minutes before slicing.
