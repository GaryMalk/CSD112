Olivier Salad

Olivier Salad, also known as Russian Potato Salad, is a traditional dish made with boiled vegetables, eggs, and mayonnaise, commonly served during holidays.

Cook time
30 minutes

Calories
260

Instructions

  1. Boil potatoes and carrots until tender.
  2. Hard boil eggs.
  3. Peel and dice potatoes, carrots, and eggs.
  4. Finely chop pickles and onions.
  5. In a large bowl, combine all ingredients.
  6. Add peas and mix gently.
  7. Season with salt and pepper.
  8. Fold in mayonnaise until well coated.
  9. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours before serving.
