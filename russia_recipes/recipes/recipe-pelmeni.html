Pelmeni

Pelmeni are traditional Russian dumplings filled with minced meat and spices, typically served with sour cream or melted butter.

Cook time
120 minutes

Calories
420

Instructions

  1. Mix flour and salt in a bowl.
  2. Add water and egg, knead into a dough.
  3. Cover and let rest for 30 minutes.
  4. For the filling, combine ground meat, onions, salt, and pepper.
  5. Roll out dough and cut into circles.
  6. Place a spoonful of filling in the center of each circle.
  7. Fold dough over filling and pinch edges to seal.
  8. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil.
  9. Drop dumplings into boiling water and cook until they float.
  10. Remove with a slotted spoon and serve with sour cream or melted butter.
