Blini

Blini are thin, crepe-like pancakes popular in Russian cuisine, often served with sour cream, caviar, or smoked salmon.

Cook time
25 minutes

Calories
80

Instructions

  1. Combine flour, salt, and baking powder in a bowl.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Mix the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients until smooth.
  4. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and lightly grease it.
  5. Pour a small amount of batter into the skillet, swirling to cover the bottom.
  6. Cook until the edges lift, then flip and cook the other side.
  7. Repeat with remaining batter.
  8. Serve warm with desired toppings.
