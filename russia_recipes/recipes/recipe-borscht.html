Borscht

Borscht is a hearty beet soup with origins in Ukraine, known for its vibrant color and tangy flavor, often served with sour cream.

Cook time
80 minutes

Calories
250

Instructions

  1. Peel and grate beets.
  2. Sauté onions and carrots in a pot until soft.
  3. Add beets, potatoes, and cabbage to the pot.
  4. Pour in vegetable broth and bring to a boil.
  5. Reduce heat and simmer until vegetables are tender.
  6. Add vinegar, sugar, and salt to taste.
  7. Stir in chopped dill and cook for an additional 5 minutes.
  8. Remove from heat and let sit for 30 minutes to allow flavors to meld.
  9. Serve hot with a dollop of sour cream.
