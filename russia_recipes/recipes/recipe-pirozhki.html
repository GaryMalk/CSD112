Pirozhki

Pirozhki are small, stuffed buns in Russian cuisine, filled with a variety of ingredients like meat, cabbage, or potatoes, and baked to golden perfection.

Cook time
120 minutes

Calories
450

Instructions

  1. Prepare dough by combining flour, yeast, sugar, salt, water, and oil.
  2. Knead dough until smooth, cover, and let rise for 1 hour.
  3. For the filling, sauté ground meat with onions until cooked through.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  6. Divide dough into small portions and roll into balls.
  7. Flatten each ball into a circle.
  8. Place a spoonful of filling in the center of each circle.
  9. Fold dough over filling and pinch edges to seal.
  10. Place buns on a baking sheet and bake for 20-25 minutes, until golden brown.
