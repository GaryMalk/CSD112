MK

MK is an interdisciplinary artist living in Seattle, Washington. Having studied at both the School of the Art Institute of Chicago as well as the Pacific Northwest College of Art and Design, you may recognize their work from prestigious locations such as this website.

Self-aware in the good ways, pretentious in all the others, their work aims to capture the realities of living with mental illness, managing loss, and an all-consuming struggle for self-identity. Their art is often messy and their visuals simplistic, reflecting an unattainable desire to capture and convey a moment's worth of thought all at once.

Currently, they work in Kirkland, Washington as an art teacher for kids and teens. Feel free to reach out to them for any questions or proposals.
