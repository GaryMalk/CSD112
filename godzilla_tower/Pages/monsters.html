Godzilla

First appearing in the original film of the franchise, 1954's Gojira, Godzilla is a monster who needs no introduction. This king of the monsters is massive, amphibious, breathes blue atomic breath, destroys cities, curshes rival monsters, and can even regenerate from individual cells in some canons. Godzilla is as ruthless as they come in combat, but has been known to form alliances with other monsters and even has a few friendships that last the majority of the Showa era. His incredible durability and regenerative power mean most human efforts to fight him are more attempts to convince him to turn around than they are serious pushes to kill him, even when the latter is their intention. This does not mean Godzilla is unbreatable, however. The king of monsters has long had a weakness to electricity, making attacks that electrocute much more affective than most. Furthermore, Godzilla has even been killed a handful of times in the series, with the most notable of these being his death in the first film to an experimental superweapon called Oxygen Destroyer.
