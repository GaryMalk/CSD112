Movies

Here you'll find a complete list of every Godzilla film with release dates and era. Godzilla films are divided into 4 Japanese eras and 2 American eras. The Japanese eras are Showa, Heisei, Millenium, and Reiwa, with the first, second, fourth being named after the eras of Japanese history they belong to (the current being Reiwa). We will also include the American version of the original film, as it is a high quality localization with reshot scenes and new characters. The American eras are Tri-Star (consisting of a single film) and Monsterverse and are distinguished by the studio behind them (Tri-Star for the first and Legendary for the second). Tracking some of these films down for streaming can be tough, but we will provide a link to a streaming location for each where possible.
