Why Japan?

The Country

Culture


One of the draws for Japan is it's rich culture. This being in part to Japan being estimated to be about 1300 years old. While there are older cultures Japan has the unique advantage of being an island country offering it much protection from being invaded meaning it's culture is well preserved and safe from war and outside influence. Exploring Japan's culture is a unique oppurtunity which is one of the reasons it's a highly popular tourist destination in the modern day.

Cities


Japan has some of the safest and cleanest cities in the world, most universities being located in or around cities this naturally a big positive. Also Japan's cities have very well maintained and functioning public transit the main drawback being how crowded it can get during peak hours, however this does mean you can go anywhere in the city without ever needing a car.

Challenge and Opportunity


Finally going to a different country provides unique oppurtunities and challenges which can lead to self growth. Japan is a great country for this due to how unique their culture is when contrasted with western countries. This point can apply to more than just Japan but is definetly worth mentioning.

The Academics

Research and Innovation


Japanese universities are among the best when it comes to engineering and technology, University of Tokyo Japan's highest ranked University is consistently cited as being among top ten world wide for physics, chemistry, and engineering. Japan being a global leader in engineering means that you don't have to make it into the top university to have access to great oppurtunities within that field.

Financially Affordable


When compared to American schools Japan's universities are incredibly cheap, going back to University of Tokyo Japan's highest ranked university costs around 5000$-8000$ usd per year depending on the program. While in local currency this can still be considered expensive for international students who are living in America and have a means of paying using western currency Japan's universities are very cheap.
