Studying in Japan

Introduction

What is This Webpage About?


The purpose of this webpage is to share what I’ve learned while researching as a prospective student who wants to study in Japan. The things I will share are what are some potential reasons why someone would want to study in Japan, where to start if you are looking to study in Japan, and what are some school choices. There will also be a page containing some extra information and resources for more information.


This page will be a mix of informed opinion and facts from other resources which should be kept in mind, always do your own research and fact checking.There will be references to more information if desired, the content will serve more a general summary to point potential readers in the right direction. Also more information will contain many of the refrences throughout the webpage in one location. As an additional final note this website is not meant as an objective opinion or source, the information provided is from the point of view of someone living in America, and not entirely unbiased in favor of Japan.
