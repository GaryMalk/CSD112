Destinations & Travel Tips

Each destination includes key highlights, transportation options, and a student budget estimate.

Istanbul

  • Highlights: Hagia Sophia, Topkapi Palace, Galata Tower, Balat
  • Transportation: Istanbulcard for bus, subway(Marmaray), and ferry
  • Budget (per day): 30–50 USD
  • Best time to visit: April–June, September–October
  • More info: Istanbul on Wikipedia

Izmir

  • Highlights: Clock Tower, Kemeraltı Bazaar, Kordon, Cable Car
  • Transportation: Izbans Subway
  • Budget (per day): 25–40 USD
  • Best time to visit: Spring and autumn
  • More info: Cappadocia on Wikipedia

Mardin

  • Highlights: Zinciriye Madrasa, Great Mosque, Dara Ancient City
  • Transportation: Mardin Airport + shuttle or taxi
  • Budget (per day): 25–40 USD
  • Best time to visit: Spring and autumn
  • More info: Cappadocia on Wikipedia

Göbeklitepe

  • Highlights: T-shaped stone pillars, visitor center
  • Transportation: 30 min drive or shuttle from Sanliurfa city center
  • Budget (per day): 20–35 USD
  • Best time to visit: Spring and autumn
  • More info: Gobeklitepe on Wikipedia

Bodrum

  • Highlights: Bodrum Castle, Mausoleum at Halikarnas, Marinas, Beaches
  • Transportation: Bodrum Airport + shuttle bus or taxi
  • Budget (per day): 40–70 USD
  • Best time to visit: Summer (June–September)
  • More info: Bodrum on Wikipedia

Kapadokya

  • Highlights: Peri Bacalari(Fairy chimneys), Goreme Open Air Museum, Hot Air Balloon Rides
  • Transportation: Nevsehir or Kayseri Airport + shuttle or bus
  • Budget (per day): 35–60 USD
  • Best time to visit: Spring and Autumn
  • More info: Cappadocia on Wikipedia
