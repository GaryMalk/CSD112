Featured Cities

This site introduces six destinations: Istanbul, Izmir, Mardin,Gobeklitepe,Bodrum and Kapadokya.

❮ ❯

Istanbul

Istanbul, with its location straddling two continents, is a unique city bearing the marks of both East and West. Exploring Hagia Sophia and Topkapi Palace in the historic peninsula while enjoying modern boulevards is unforgettable. A ferry tour along the Bosphorus is the best way to see the city from the water.

❮ ❯

Izmir

Izmir, the pearl of the Aegean, is famous for its coastline and friendly people. Stroll along the Kordon and shop affordably in the Kemeraltı Bazaar. The Clock Tower, a city landmark, is a must-see for every visitor.

❮ ❯

Mardin

With its stone houses and narrow streets, Mardin feels like time travel. The Zinciriye Madrasa and the Grand Mosque give the city a historical identity. The Ancient City of Dara is a fascinating stopover carrying traces of Mesopotamian civilizations.

Gobeklitepe

Gobeklitepe left its mark on archaeological history as the world’s oldest known temple complex. Its T-shaped stone columns shed light on a 12,000-year-old past. Visitors can discover the oldest traces of belief and life in human history here.

❮ ❯

Bodrum

Bodrum, with its white houses, azure bays, and vibrant nightlife, is one of the jewels of the Aegean. Bodrum Castle and the Ancient Theatre are a should see.

❮ ❯

Kapadokya

Cappadocia is famous for its Peri Bacalari(fairy chimneys), underground cities, and balloon tours. Watching the sunrise from atop a balloon is an unforgettable experience.
