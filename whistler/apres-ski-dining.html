Good Places to Grab a Bite

Sushi Village

Known for its lively atmosphere, Sushi Village is a popular spot for après-ski sushi and sake margaritas. Located in the heart of Whistler Village, this restaurant offers a mix of traditional and creative sushi options, along with Japanese-inspired dishes that pair perfectly with the après vibe.

Merlin's

Located at the base of Blackcomb Mountain, Merlin's is a classic après-ski bar with a laid-back vibe and a famous nacho platter. Known for live music and a friendly crowd, it's a great spot to kick back after a day on the slopes, especially if you're looking for a more relaxed, ski-in vibe.

Dubh Linn Gate Irish Pub

This cozy Irish pub brings a bit of Dublin to Whistler. With hearty food, an extensive beer selection, and often live music, Dubh Linn Gate offers a warm, lively setting for après. Their pub fare, especially the shepherd’s pie and fish & chips, is highly recommended.

Garibaldi Lift Co. (GLC) Lounge

The GLC Lounge, situated at the base of Whistler Mountain, provides fantastic mountain views and a lively après scene. Known for their cocktails and upscale pub fare, the GLC is ideal for both a relaxed meal or an energetic après-ski gathering with friends.
