Discover Whistler's Ski Runs and Mountains

Whistler Mountain

Whistler Mountain is the perfect destination for skiers of all levels, offering a wide variety of terrain to suit both beginners and experts. New skiers can enjoy the abundance of green circle (easy) runs, which are well-groomed, wide, and gently sloping, allowing for a comfortable learning experience. The mountain’s diverse runs ensure that beginners can build confidence while still having access to more challenging terrain as they improve their skills. With a range of runs to explore, Whistler is ideal for skiers who want to progress at their own pace. For advanced skiers, Whistler offers plenty of thrilling challenges, including steep black diamond runs, mogul fields, and off-piste terrain. One of the most iconic runs is Peak to Creek, which takes skiers from the peak of Whistler Mountain all the way down to the base, offering a mix of terrain and an unforgettable experience. The Peak to Peak Gondola, connecting Whistler to Blackcomb Mountain, provides breathtaking views and easy access to even more terrain, making it one of the most diverse and scenic ski destinations in the world. Whether you're learning or seeking adventure, Whistler has something for everyone..

Blackcomb Mountain

Blackcomb Mountain is renowned for its challenging runs and expansive terrain, making it a favorite for experienced skiers and snowboarders. The mountain offers a wide variety of slopes, from steep black diamond trails to expansive alpine bowls, providing something for everyone. Known for its impressive terrain parks, Blackcomb is also one of the top destinations in North America for freestylers looking for big jumps, rails, and unique features. Whether you're seeking adrenaline-pumping descents or just a scenic alpine experience, Blackcomb delivers it all. Two of Blackcomb's standout chairlifts are Seventh Heaven and Jersey Cream, both offering access to some of the mountain's best terrain. Seventh Heaven takes you to some of the highest and most challenging alpine terrain, with panoramic views and powder-filled bowls that skiers love to explore. Meanwhile, Jersey Cream offers access to a mix of intermediate and expert runs, including some of the mountain’s famous tree runs. These chairlifts provide easy access to some of the most thrilling skiing on Blackcomb, ensuring that adventurers can make the most of this iconic mountain. Explore Blackcomb Mountain.

Peak to Peak Gondola

The Peak to Peak Gondola is an iconic engineering marvel that holds the record for the highest lift in the world and the longest lift span between two towers. It connects Whistler and Blackcomb Mountains, providing skiers and snowboarders with a seamless experience of both mountains. The gondola offers breathtaking views of the surrounding alpine scenery, making it a must-do experience whether you're skiing or simply enjoying the views. Riding the Peak to Peak Gondola is not just about getting to the slopes; it's a stunning journey with panoramic views. In addition to the usual ski season, the gondola operates during the summer months, providing hikers and tourists access to stunning alpine trails and vistas. It's an incredible way to experience the natural beauty of Whistler, offering views of glaciers, lakes, and towering peaks.
