Where to Stay in Whistler

Club Interwest

Club Interwest has been my home away from home for the past 22 years, and it remains my favorite place to stay in Whistler. Located in the heart of the village, it offers the perfect blend of comfort, convenience, and family-friendly amenities. The spacious suites come with fully equipped kitchens, making it easy to enjoy meals in or relax after a day on the slopes. The property is packed with amenities that make each stay enjoyable, including an arcade, a small market, a movie theater, a gym, and a steam room. For families or those seeking some fun after skiing, there's something for everyone. One of the highlights of Club Interwest is its two pools: one with a water slide for kids and the other with two hot tubs for adults, offering a perfect way to unwind after a long day. The location also provides easy access to Whistler Village and the slopes, making it a convenient choice for skiers and snowboarders. The ski storage and fitness facilities further enhance the experience, making it a great place for relaxation and recreation. Whether you're visiting for a weekend or staying for longer, Club Interwest ensures a memorable and comfortable stay. Club Interwest on VRBO.

Fairmont Chateau Whistler

The Fairmont Chateau Whistler is an iconic luxury resort, offering ski-in/ski-out access at the base of Blackcomb Mountain. With five-star service, elegant rooms, a world-class spa, and fine dining, it’s the ultimate choice for a luxurious stay in Whistler. Perfect for those looking for both relaxation and adventure.

The Westin Resort & Spa

Located in Whistler Village, The Westin offers cozy rooms, a luxurious spa, and easy access to both the slopes and village amenities. Ideal for those seeking a blend of relaxation and adventure, it provides an excellent location for skiing, dining, and après-ski fun.

Blackcomb Lodge

Perfect for those seeking a quieter, more relaxed atmosphere, Blackcomb Lodge offers comfortable accommodations with a convenient location. Just steps from the lifts and Whistler Village, this lodge provides a cozy retreat with easy access to all the action.
