IPHONE 14 PRO

FEATURES

All models are equipped with the A16 Bionic chip, a 48-megapixel main camera, and ProMotion displays with Ceramic Shield protection and a frequency range of 1 — 120 Hz, as well as accelerated LPDDR5 memory. The design of the lineup has been updated, with two notches on the front panel of the smartphones.

All iPhone 14 models in the USA will be sold without a SIM card tray: the manufacturer claims that the improved eSIM technology will allow transferring old electronic SIM cards to new smartphones.

The front camera has been updated (it is equipped with autofocus and recognizes the user faster in low light conditions) as well as the sensor of the main camera.

The iPhone 14 Pro has been released in a new design — without the signature "notch". Also, a new color has been added to the assortment. Unlike the "regular" iPhone 14 models, the smartphone is equipped with the A16 Bionic processor, which can perform 4 trillion operations with photos per second.
