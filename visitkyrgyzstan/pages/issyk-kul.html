Issyk‑Kul Lake

Issyk‑Kul is a large alpine lake famous for its sandy beaches and warm summer weather. It never freezes because of its depth and salt content.

Best time: June – September. How to get there: ~4 hours by car from Bishkek to Cholpon‑Ata.

Tips: Bring sunscreen, swim shoes, and cash for beach rentals.
