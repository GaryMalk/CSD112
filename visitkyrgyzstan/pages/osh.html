Osh City

Osh is one of the oldest cities in Central Asia. Visit the Jayma Bazaar and climb Sulaiman‑Too for city views and history.

Best time: April – October. Getting there: 1‑hour flight from Bishkek or a long scenic road trip.

Tips: Try Osh plov, carry small bills, and dress modestly at religious sites.
