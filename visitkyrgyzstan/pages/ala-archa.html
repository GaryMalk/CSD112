Ala‑Archa National Park

Ala‑Archa is the quickest way to see high mountains near Bishkek. Trails range from easy riverside walks to steep routes to glaciers.

Best time: May – October. Getting there: 40 km south of Bishkek by taxi or tour.

Tips: Wear hiking shoes, bring water, and check weather before you go.
