The Megastructure

The Megastructure is not exactly a place fit for human habitation. It used to serve humans, sure, but the power has long since run out, machinery that used to work has seized, and thus toxic chemicals pour over some areas, while exploded machinery litters others. Some animals have managed to make refuge here, but only the most resourceful of them. Perhaps you'll be resourceful enough to survive here, too.
