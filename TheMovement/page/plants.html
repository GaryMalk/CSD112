Plants

Bounce Mushrooms

Found a ledge that's too tall to reach? Bounce Mushrooms will spring you up into the air to reach higher altitudes!

Zapper Sunflowers

The Megastructure is full of ancient, broken down machinery. Use these bright sunflowers to zap them full of solar-powered electricity and bring them back to life!

Purifying Lilies

Sadly, much of the water in The Megastructure has long been contaminated with toxins and sludge. These lilies are incredibly hardy and can clean up the mess, making room for new plants!
