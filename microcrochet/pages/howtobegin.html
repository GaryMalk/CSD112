How do I Begin Micro-crocheting?

Step 1: Choose your design
So, you've decided to micro-crochet! How do I start? Step 1 involves choosing a template or model. Picking an animal or item you want to make is also okay. Then search for a pattern. Once you find the pattern, you may need to buy it or it may be free.

Step 2: Start reading your pattern
Now that you have a pattern on hand, you can start reading it! Follow the stitches, step by step, just like in normal crochet! For materials, check out this page

Step 3: Put all the pieces together
Now that you have finished crocheting the small pieces for your project (if the project involves pieces. If not, skip to Step 4), you an start putting them together. If the pieces have wires in them, add a dab of glue where you want to glue the pieces and stick the wire in. For non-wired pieces, either sew or glue the piece on.

Step 4: Color your micro-crochet project
Use either acrylic colors with a mix or use water colors. Go slow and gentle, you don't want to ruin your work! If you mess up with watercolor you can dip the project in water and let the color soak out!
