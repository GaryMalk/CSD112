Microcrochet Patterns

Microcrochet is rewarding, and is especially fun when you get to design your own creations. However, many people, (including me when I first started) find it especially hard to make something, especially for those who are struggling to get their first stiches in. This is why many microcrochet-ers use patterns. Patterns are essentially roadmaps for your project, and use symbols and shorthand along with diagrams to guide you on you journey (The shorthands and stiches are explained here )

Many websites offer free or paid microcrochet patterns, but my favorite website to go it is Pinterest.com
