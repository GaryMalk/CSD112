Why Should I Microcrochet?

You can't talk about microcrochet without talking about crochet first, and crochet is a very pleasurable hobby to do for many people, with me being no exception! Crochet can acually lower stress and improve dexterity, and can be done in many places, not just at home. For example, crocheting in public spaces or when you are stressed can help pass time and relive stress. In addition, crochet is a relatively cheap and easy to learn hobby! Crochet also gives you a sense of accomplishmet, because you cna acually make useful items like bags or clothing, but also cute stuffies or gifts.

Microcrochet has all of the benefits of crochet, but it is harder to master and can take more time. Microcrochet crafts, as I talked about in the Home page, can sell for high prices on the market, and are sought for due to their complexity and the plethora of details packed into such a small object. This is further enhanced as microcrochet employes some of the much more complicated stiches in crochet. Microcrochet requires mastering of:

  • Basic to Intermediate crochet stiches
  • Patience and Organization of the small parts
  • Ability to read a crochet pattern
  • (Optional) Knowledge of colors and dyes
  • (Optional) Knowledge of how to use paint brushes and other coloring tools

With knowledge of the above skills, you will be able create beautiful microcrochet stuffies and items!
