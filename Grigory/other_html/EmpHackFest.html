EMP Hackfest

Emerald Parents hosted HackFest

EMP Hackfest was a hackathon hosted for the high school students of the region around King County. The Hackathon was hosted on the Redmond Microsoft Campus, with sponsor awards ranging up to 500$. My team consisted of me and one other colleague, who\ ended up winning the largest prize available at the hackathon.

The hackathon project we submitted was called volunteer me, which was a internet scrapper designed to scrape the internet for volunteer oppurtunities that aren't posted on other volunteer websites, i.e. goverment volunteering.
