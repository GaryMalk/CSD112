TSA

Technology Student Association

The Technology Student Association (TSA) is a national organization for middle and high schoolers interested in technology and engineering. More then 2000 schools in 49 states have TSA Chapters, basically clubs that go on to compete at the state and national level. All chapters have their own teams to send to such events, one of which was my team.

My team, including myself and two other students, won the competition for architectural design in our chapter, went on to place top 5 statewide, and also competeted at the national level. While this experience did result in me gaining valuable experience in CAD, my biggest lesson was how to push myself and my team for the best results.
