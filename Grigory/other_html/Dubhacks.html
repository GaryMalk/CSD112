DubHacks Hackathon

DubHacks was a Hackathon hosted by the University of Washington-Seattle, intended participants were computer science students from around the state. DubHacks was a 2 day hackathon hosted on UW's campus with a coveted panel of judges grading each team's project.

My team developed "Shield Daddy", a web extension designed to detect the user trying to access a phishy site BEFORE the user actually enters such site. Skills developed included technical skills such as css, html, node.js. However the most important skill learned was being able to collaborate with a team under a deadline.
