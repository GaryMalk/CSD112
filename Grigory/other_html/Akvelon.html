Akvelon

Refactoring PowerBI Visuals

In the summer of 2023, I interned for a company called Akvelon. Akvelon is a technology consulting and software development company that provides services including custom software development, IT consulting, and project management. During my internship at Akvelon, my team worked on refactoring old/less popular powerbi visuals.

Due to the fact that our team was actively trying to improve these visuals, our work resulted in a lot of collaboration and communication with the official microsoft powerbi team. Through an intense 12 week internship, my team and I managed to push 4 visuals to production, including our visuals being used 40% more after our work. Skills gained includes technical skills in javascript and typescript, as well as experience working with azure cloud services.
