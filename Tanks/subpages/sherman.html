Sherman Firefly (VC)

Sherman Firefly, as mentioned in the previous description, was a modified Sherman M4, but unlike M4 that we are familiar of, Firefly is one of the most devastating Ally tank out there. Why? Becuase it is always able to penetrate the frontal armor of a Panther or Tiger tank, which, at the time, no other ally tanks can constantly do so.
