T-34

Just like the Panzer IV, the T-34 has many different variations and models, and they get better as the war progresses

  1. Model 1940: the original T-34, with a 76.2mm gun
  2. Model 1943: the iconic hexagonal turret
  3. Model 1943: the original T-34-85, the brand new powerful T-34 mounted with a 85 mm gun
  4. Model 1969: Model T-34-85M, refurbishing the old T-34-85 with new equipments
